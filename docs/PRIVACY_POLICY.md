# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-16

QuackDoku is a mobile puzzle game. This policy explains what information the app collects and how it is used.

## Information We Collect

QuackDoku may collect:

- Gameplay progress stored on the device, such as completed cases, coins, clues, daily results, selected character and tutorial state.
- Analytics events, such as app opens, screen views, case starts, case completions, hint usage and shop/profile/character screen visits.
- Basic technical information included with analytics requests, such as app environment, release, timestamps and anonymous user identifiers.
- Daily leaderboard submissions, such as username, anonymous install identifier, stars, completion time, errors and completion date.
- Push notification token and notification preference, if the user enables daily reminders.

The current app does not require an account and does not ask for an email address. Purchases, when enabled, are processed by the platform store and RevenueCat; QuackDoku does not receive full payment card information.

## How We Use Information

We use the information to:

- Save local game progress.
- Understand which parts of the game are used.
- Find bugs and improve performance.
- Balance difficulty, rewards and onboarding.
- Operate the daily leaderboard.
- Send daily reminders if enabled by the user.

## Analytics

QuackDoku uses PostHog for product analytics. Analytics events are sent with an anonymous identifier unless the app later adds an account system. PostHog may process technical metadata needed to receive and analyze events.

## Data Stored On Your Device

QuackDoku stores gameplay progress locally using device storage. Removing the app may remove local progress, depending on the device and platform behavior.

## Advertising

QuackDoku may use Google AdMob for rewarded ads. Rewarded ads are optional and may be used to unlock in-game rewards such as clues or continues. AdMob may process device and advertising identifiers according to Google's policies.

## Purchases

QuackDoku may use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchase entitlements. Purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. RevenueCat may receive purchase identifiers, entitlement status and anonymous app user identifiers needed to validate access.

## Push Notifications

If the user enables daily reminders, QuackDoku may request notification permission and store an Expo push token. The user can disable reminders from the app settings or system settings.

## Children

QuackDoku is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If a parent or guardian believes a child provided personal information, they can contact us to request deletion.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Analytics data may be processed by service providers used to operate and improve the app.
